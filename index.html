Brief History

Welcome to our school

In June 1999, the idea of school was birthed through a young girl met at Christ Apostolic Church. The encounter led to my employment at Federal Ministry of Works and Housing building, Iponri to meet a family's educational need. After about a year, I started SHALOM NURSERY AND TUTORIALS. Later we moved to 2 Afric road Iponri in the year 2000 to run a Nursery and Primary School named REHOBOTH NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOLS.

We later relocated to 1 Moshalashi street Iponri in 2006, then the school experienced a massive boost in population and organizational structure and grew to over 80 pupils. We enjoyed the services of very hard-working, and committed teachers who later became Head teachers. Notable among them are Mustapha Omowunmi (Mrs), Oladele Micheal and. Adigun Omolabake (Miss). We relocated in September 2021 to No 3 Oke Olu street Iponri.
